Golden Grain

Golden Grain Bakery and Cafe opened its doors in 2008 in the heart of Glendale and since that time, it continues to delight the customers with delicious, healthy food and the aroma of invigorating coffee. Once you get inside the bakery and feel the cozy atmosphere, Golden Grain will become your favorite place to spend your time with family and friends. Our specialists work with the goal that the portion of bread you purchase toward the beginning of the day is crisp out of the stove! Our bakery offers indoor and outdoor seating area, however you can use our delivery or take away services as well. Spreading bliss and commending each snapshot of life is the key factor in Golden Grains prosperity.
